Accessible+Connected Community

An accessible and connected community offers and encourages a variety of safe, accessible and sustainable mobility options. It plans, designs and maintains effective infrastructure networks, while supporting strong regional multimodal connections. It provides open access to information, encourages innovation, enhances communication and promotes community engagement. Lastly it balances a transportation system that reflects effective land use.

Good Governance

A community focused on Good Governance strives toward sustainability of the town’s financial, human, information, and physical assets. It supports strategic decision-making with timely, reliable and accurate data and analysis and works to enhance transparency, accuracy, efficiency, effectiveness and quality customer service in all business. It supports, develops and enhances relationships between the town and community/regional partners and provides assurance of regulatory and policy compliance.

Harmony with Nature

A community in harmony with nature supports and sustains natural resource and energy conservation. It promotes and regulates an ecologically balanced community and works to mitigate and abate threats to the environment.

Healthy+Socially Responsible Community

A healthy and socially responsible community cultivates a wide-range of recreational, cultural educational, and social opportunities and supports the physical and mental well-being of its community members. It actively partners with others to improve the welfare of those in need, to foster inclusion, to embrace diversity and respect human rights. It enhances multi-generational community enrichment and community engagement.

Livable Community

A livable community is one that promotes and sustains a safe, clean and attractive place to live, work and play and facilitates housing options to accommodate a diverse community. It provides safe and well-maintained public infrastructure, and provides adequate and appropriate regulation of development resources. It encourages sustainable development supported by reliable and affordable town services and supports and enhances neighborhood livability for all members of the community.

Vital+Resilient Economy

A community with a vital and resilient economy supports an environment for creativity and innovation, and invests in infrastructure and amenities that attract, sustain and retain diverse businesses. It promotes a qualified and diversified work force that meets employers’ needs and supports broad-based economic diversity. It fosters regional and public/private collaboration with key institutions and organizations that contribute to economic sustainability.

About the Comp Plan

The Town's current Comprehensive Plan that guides much of what we do in Lexington is a 15-year old document. Lexington is a much different town than it was 15 years ago. We have grown in many ways and face many future challenges as well as opportunities and hope to get your input as we move forward. The Planning Board, Comprehensive Plan Advisory Committee, and Planning Staff have a robust process in place to achieve an update to the Comprehensive Plan that will help guide us through the next 5 to 10-year period. The Comprehensive Plan process has been underway since last summer. The Planning Board has created a well-rounded, 13-person, citizen-based Comprehensive Plan Advisory Committee to be a conduit for a robust public process.
